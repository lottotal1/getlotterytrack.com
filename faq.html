Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started. Can't find your answer? Email us.

Getting Started

Do I need any special equipment? +
No. Just an iPhone running iOS 15 or later. The barcode scanner uses your phone's built-in camera — no external hardware, no accessories, nothing to buy. If you already own an iPhone, you're ready to start.
How long does it take to set up? +
Most store owners are up and running in under 10 minutes. You'll create your account, enter your store information, and you're ready to close. No complex configuration, no importing spreadsheets, no training required.
Do my employees need their own accounts? +
Your Starter plan includes up to 2 employee accounts per store. Each employee gets their own login so that every closing session is attributed to the right person. This is how you maintain an accurate audit trail. Need more employee accounts? Contact us.
Does it work for all NY scratch-off tickets? +
Yes. Lottery Track comes pre-loaded with all 108 active New York Lottery scratch-off games. The app understands the NY Lottery barcode format exactly — game number, ticket denomination, and book structure — so every scan is recognized correctly from day one.

Using the App

What if a barcode won't scan? +
If a barcode is damaged, torn, or won't scan for any reason, you can enter the ticket information manually. Every scan has a manual entry fallback — just tap the manual entry option and type in the game and book number. No close is ever blocked by a bad barcode.
Does it work without internet? +
Yes. Lottery Track works fully offline. You can scan barcodes, enter your 3 numbers, and complete a full closing session without any internet connection. Your data is saved locally on your device and automatically synced to the cloud the next time you reconnect.
Can two employees use the app at the same time? +
Each store supports one active closing session at a time. This ensures your inventory data stays consistent and accurate. Two employees cannot close simultaneously for the same store — but they can use the app on different devices for viewing history and reports.
What if I scan the wrong ticket? +
No problem. Before you complete the closing session, you can review your full inventory on the inventory screen and delete any item that was scanned by mistake. Just tap the item and remove it. Changes take effect immediately before the close is finalized.
What are the 3 numbers I need to enter? +
The three numbers come directly from your NY Lottery terminal at the end of each day:
  • New Books Activated — the total value of any new books you activated during the shift
  • Online Summary Balance — your total online (numbers) game sales for the day
  • Instant Validation Payouts — the total value of scratch-off winnings you paid out

Billing and Account

Is there a free trial? +
Yes. Every new account gets a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to start. At the end of your trial, you'll be prompted to subscribe if you'd like to continue.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Lottery Track is month-to-month with no contracts or cancellation fees. You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. During this time, you can export your closing history as PDFs. After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted. We recommend downloading your statements before cancelling if you want to keep them for your records.
Do you offer discounts for multiple stores? +
Yes. If you own 2 or more stores, you qualify for our Multi-Store rate of $12.99 per store per month. For owners with 5 or more locations, contact us at support@getlotterytrack.com for custom pricing.

Security and Privacy

Is my data secure? +
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). We use Supabase infrastructure, which is trusted by thousands of businesses and meets SOC 2 compliance standards. Row-level security policies ensure your store's data is completely isolated from any other account.
Do you sell my data? +
Never. We do not sell, rent, share, or monetize your business data in any way. Your closing numbers, inventory records, and store information are used only to provide you with the Lottery Track service. We use Supabase for database hosting and Resend for email — these are our only third-party data processors.
Who can see my closing data? +
Only authorized users in your store can see your closing data. Store owners have full access to all sessions. Employees can see sessions they participated in within their store. No one outside your store — including Lottery Track staff — accesses your business data except for the purpose of troubleshooting a specific issue you've reported.
