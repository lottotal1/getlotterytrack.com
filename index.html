How It Works

Five steps. Any employee can learn in one shift.

📷
1

Scan your books

Point your camera at the barcode on each open scratch-off book. The app reads the game name, price, and book number instantly.

📋
2

Check the list

Review every open book in today's scan. If a customer bought a ticket mid-scan, tap the row to update the count — or just re-scan the book.

✅
3

Close the day

Enter New Books Activated, your Online Summary Balance, and any cash Payouts. That's it — three numbers.

📊
4

Get your statement

Lottery Track calculates your Total Cash Balance and generates a professional PDF you can save, share, or send to your accountant.

👥
5

Your team

Add employees in Settings. Each person gets their own login — so every closing is tied to a name, a shift, and a timestamp.

No training needed. If your employees can use a phone camera, they can use Lottery Track.

OUR STORY

Lottery Track didn't start in a boardroom. It started behind a counter, watching employees spend 20 minutes every night doing the same repetitive count — book by book, ticket by ticket, adding it all up on a calculator, sometimes having to start over because of a mistake.

We knew there had to be a better way. The barcode is right there on every book. The math is the same every night. So we built the tool that should have existed years ago.

We're not a big tech company trying to sell you software. We're people who understand what it takes to run a retail store in New York City — the long hours, the tight margins, the pressure to do everything right. Lottery Track is the tool we wish we'd had.
