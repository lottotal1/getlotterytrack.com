Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Lottery Track ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Lottery Track mobile application and related services ("the Service"). By using Lottery Track, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

  • Account information: Your email address, username, and full name, provided when you create an account.
  • Store information: Store name, address, and state, entered during setup.
  • Business data: Lottery ticket inventory counts, daily closing totals, New Books Activated amounts, Online Summary Balance figures, and Instant Validation Payout amounts that you enter into the app.
  • Session data: Timestamps and employee attribution for each closing session you complete.
  • Usage data: App activity logs used to diagnose errors and improve performance. These do not include the content of your business data.
  • Device information: Device type and operating system version, used for crash diagnostics only.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide, maintain, and improve the Lottery Track service. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Lottery Track service
  • Generate closing statements, inventory reports, and daily PDF summaries within the app
  • Send alert notifications you have opted into (such as session completion confirmations)
  • Respond to support requests and bug reports
  • Improve the app based on aggregate usage patterns
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with industry-standard encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS). We use row-level security (RLS) policies to ensure each user can only access their own store's data. This means your closing data is completely isolated from every other account on the platform.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use Supabase for database hosting and Resend for transactional email delivery. These providers process data only as necessary to provide their contracted services, and are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Lottery Track, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. We will notify you of any such change.

5. Employee Accounts

Store owners can create employee accounts within their store. Employees can access inventory scanning and closing features scoped to their assigned store. Store owners can view all activity performed by their employees within the app, including closing session details, timestamps, and attributed data.

If you are an employee using Lottery Track, you should be aware that your store owner has visibility into your activity within the app. This is a core feature of the audit trail functionality.

6. Data Retention

We retain your business data — including closing statements, inventory records, and session history — for as long as your account is active. We maintain 30 days of rolling session history as part of the standard service.

After account cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days, during which time you can export your records. After 30 days, your account data is permanently deleted from our systems.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at admin@getlotterytrack.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications at any time
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format upon request

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@getlotterytrack.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Lottery Track is intended for use by adults (18 years of age or older) operating authorized retail lottery businesses. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a minor without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we may also send a notification to the email address associated with your account.

Your continued use of Lottery Track after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: admin@getlotterytrack.com
  • Website: getlotterytrack.com/contact

We will respond to privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
